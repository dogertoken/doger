Whitepaper
Fair Token Minting System:

Open Minting: DOGER tokens can be minted by anyone who complies with the specified terms and conditions. Each minting is carried out with clear and transparent proportions: each minting received will produce a certain number of tokens based on a set amount.

Automatic Token Burning (Burn Mechanism):

Burn Percentage: Every time DOGER tokens are moved between users, a small percentage of the tokens are burned automatically. 1% of each transfer will be burned, reducing the total amount of supply circulating in the market. Supply Reduction: Token burning aims to reduce supply over time, which can create a deflationary effect and help maintain token value in the long term.

Manual Burn Function & Total Limited Supply (Max Supply):

Manual Burn: Users can burn their tokens manually via the burn function. This gives users more control over the number of tokens they own and allows them to participate in the reduction of existing supply.

Total Limited Supply (Max Supply) Maximum Supply Limit: Doger Portal has a maximum supply limit of 10 billion DOGER. Once this limit is reached, no more tokens can be demanded, providing assurance that there will be no excessive inflation in the token supply.

Transparency and Security:

With a clear and open tokenomics structure, Doger Portal provides incentives for the community to actively participate in the ecosystem, whether in the form of minting, burning, or simply supporting projects within the community. Distribution to the Community: 100% of the token supply will be distributed to the community through a fair launch, giving anyone the opportunity to join and participate in the project without any allocation to developers or early investors.
